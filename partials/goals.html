Goals & Objectives

Where We Are Heading

Our strategic goals guide our growth trajectory and keep us focused on delivering value.

01

Global Recognition

To be accepted not only locally but globally as a formidable detergents producer.

02

Fair Competition

To compete in the market fairly with other established businesses in the industry.

03

Employment Creation

To create employment and become a key player in Kenya's economy.

04

Empowerment

To empower the youth and women to venture into business and embrace self-employment.

05

Sustainable Growth

To make profits and enjoy the benefits that come with operating a stable business plant.
