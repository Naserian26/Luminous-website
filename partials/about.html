Our Story & Heritage

From a small entrepreneurial venture to a registered limited company, our journey reflects dedication, hard work, and an unwavering commitment to quality.

2018 Established

Building Since Day One

We started as an entrepreneurial project with two directors who did everything themselves — from manufacturing and marketing to supplying products. Working under the name 'Luminous Ventures', we sold our products to local supermarkets, schools, and restaurants.

With time, we acquired the resources needed for formal registration. On the 6th of August 2018, under The Company's Act 2015, Luminous Products Limited was born — a private limited company managed by its founder figures and directors.
